Carrot thing recipe

    Ingredients

  • Carrots 500gr
  • Butter
  • Milk(3%) 1 litre
  • Sugar 100-200gr
  • Nut mix chopped
  • Pinch of cardamum powder

    how to make it

  1. Peel the carrots and shred them with grater
  2. Add 50-100gr butter in thick hot pan and let it become clear
  3. Add shredded carrot in the butter and cook them until they become soft and fragrant
  4. Add milk into the carrots and cook until milk evaporates and you left with soft mushy mixer, don't forget to keep stirring
  5. Then add sugar and keep mixing until sugar dissolves and there is no moisture left from sugar
  6. Add pinch of cardamum powder and chopped nut mix
Viola! your dessert is ready :)))
